### Why This Title (Not "Cowboys vs Indians")

After reading the full manuscript, the original title is a complete mismatch.
This book is NOT about cowboys fighting Native Americans. It is a metaphysical
thriller about a female deputy who astral projects to escape abuse, battles a
dark entity whose unresolved trauma is poisoning her town, and ultimately
sacrifices herself across dimensions to restore quantum balance.

"Beyond the Veil" is the right title because:
- "The veil" is universally understood as the boundary between the living and
  the dead / the physical and the spiritual, deeply resonant given the
  dedication to your brother who crossed over Sep 3, 2024
- It is search-friendly on Amazon for paranormal + spiritual fiction readers
- "Quantum Western Thriller" as a subtitle is a completely unique genre tag --
  there is nothing else on Amazon that says this, which is a discoverability advantage
- Series branding ("Hailey Pink Chronicles") sets up future books immediately

## BOOK DESCRIPTION / BLURB (Amazon Product Page)

She escapes her bruises every night by leaving her body behind.

Deputy Hailey Pink is trapped. Trapped in a dying Western town where the
sheriff laughs at her warnings. Trapped in a home where her boyfriend's fists
speak louder than her badge. But every time she closes her eyes and sleeps --
she flies.

In the astral realm, she is untouchable. She swims with ancient creatures in
oceans of liquid light. She soars over floating islands of glowing forest.
She is powerful in ways this world will never allow her to be.

But something is following her there.

A shadow. A presence. A dark and ancient rage that does not belong to this
world, or any other she has ever known.

When Hailey's town begins to fall apart, people drifting through the streets
like hollow shells, a sickness no doctor can name, a silence that feels like
something feeding, she starts to understand. The astral realm is not just a
dream. It is real. The worlds are connected. And whatever is hunting her in
the dark is bleeding into her waking life and destroying everyone she has
sworn to protect.

The truth, when she finds it, is stranger than any ghost story:
The entity is not a monster. It is a man, in another dimension, who does
not even know he is doing it. His unresolved anger. His unhealed wounds. His
consciousness, leaking across the quantum web, poisoning her world like a
slow and invisible plague.

To stop him, Hailey will have to become something the frontier has never seen:
a warrior of the mind, a healer of broken dimensions, and a woman willing to
pay the ultimate price.

Beyond the Veil is a quantum Western thriller about what happens when
consciousness becomes the battlefield, and healing one soul might cost you
everything.

"The real battle will be fought inside you."

Genre: Paranormal / Metaphysical Fiction / Western Thriller
Themes: Astral projection, quantum consciousness, domestic abuse survival,
female empowerment, sacrifice, parallel timelines, spiritual healing
Tone: Lyrical and dreamlike in the astral realm. Gritty and raw in the
Western world. Fast-paced and cinematic in the battles.
Comparable Authors: Haruki Murakami meets Cormac McCarthy meets Colleen Hoover
Target Readers: Fans of Station Eleven, The Midnight Library, The Dark Tower
