Payment: Stripe microtransactions (same Stripe account as ebooks)

Implementation: Each game loads in its own iframe container.
Lazy loading. Aspect ratio containers. Full-screen toggle.
Mobile responsive. Both games share the same session and wallet system.

Game session flow:
  1. First visit: cookie/localStorage tracks "free game used" (per game)
  2. After free game ends, overlay appears: "INSERT COIN -- $0.25 for 3 lives"
  3. Stripe Checkout opens for the microtransaction
  4. On success, Supabase stores a session token with 3 lives
  5. Lives decrement on each game-over. When 0, back to the pay wall.
  6. Price compounds per the tier above (tracked in localStorage + Supabase)
  7. Lives are per-game. Day Pass and VIP cover all games.

## GAME 2: EVERLIGHT BLACKJACK (/arcade/blackjack)

Not your grandfather's blackjack. The house still has an edge.
You just have more ways to press it.

8-deck shoe. Dealer stands on soft 17. Standard blackjack pays 3:2.
Insurance pays 2:1. That is where normal ends.

DOUBLE DOWN MADNESS:
Double down on ANY card count. Not just 9, 10, or 11 -- any total.
The option stays available after every hit. You see a card you like,
you double. Doubles the current bet and deals exactly one more card.
Reckless players will love this. Smart players will weaponize it.

SPLIT RULES:
- Aces: split up to 4 times (4 hands from one pair of Aces)
- Eights: split up to 4 times
- All other pairs: split up to 2 times
- Double down allowed after splitting on any split hand
- Each split hand plays independently with its own bet

WAR BET (side bet):
Before the deal, place an optional War Bet. Your second card goes
head-to-head against the dealer's visible card. Higher card wins.
Ties push. Pays 2:1. Simple. Volatile. Addictive.

VISUAL DESIGN:
- Dark casino felt background (#0D1117) with subtle texture grain
- Chip animations on bet placement (stack, slide, click sounds)
- Card flip animations with realistic physics
- Gold particle burst on wins (scales with payout size)
- Neon flash and screen shake on natural blackjack (21 on first two cards)
- Ambient casino soundscape with mute button (top right, always visible)
- Chip denominations: $1 (white), $5 (red), $10 (blue), $25 (green), $100 (black), $500 (purple)
- Bet display: current bet, bank balance, session profit/loss
- Card count: not displayed, but the shoe is honest -- 8 decks, no reshuffle until 75% penetration

Implementation: Canvas-based game in iframe. Same session system
as Alley Kingz. Shares player account, wallet, and arcade pricing.
