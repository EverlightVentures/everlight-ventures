## SECTION 2: THE PROBLEM

**Section Label:**
The problem

**Headline:**
You have AI tools. You do not have an AI team.

**Body:**
Right now you are the middleware. You copy a strategy from ChatGPT, paste it into Claude for refinement, switch to Codex for implementation, then manually track what happened where. You are managing four browser tabs, three chat histories, and zero shared context between any of them.

Every AI starts from scratch. None of them know what the others said. None of them remember what you decided yesterday. You are coordinating a team of brilliant specialists using sticky notes and willpower.

This does not scale. And you already know it.

## SECTION 3: THE SOLUTION

**Section Label:**
The platform

**Headline:**
One command. Your entire AI team mobilizes.

**Body:**
Hive Mind routes your intent to the right AI agents, runs them in parallel, and converges their output into a single actionable result. Each agent plays to its strength. You stay in the driver's seat.

**Feature Grid (2x3 layout, dark cards with violet icon accents):**

**War Room**
Multi-agent strategy sessions in real time. Claude plans, Gemini executes, Codex engineers, Perplexity researches -- all on one screen. Debate logging and consensus scoring built in.

**Smart Router**
Describe what you need in plain language. The router classifies your intent and engages only the agents that matter. Trading question? Claude + Codex + Perplexity. Content job? Claude + Gemini + Perplexity. No wasted tokens.

**Taskboard**
AI-to-human handoff system. Every deliverable surfaces as an actionable task -- approve, revise, or delegate. Nothing falls through the cracks between agent output and your execution.

**Nerve Center**
Real-time system health monitoring. See which agents are active, what they are working on, session history, token usage, and performance analytics. Full operational visibility.

**Content Factory**
Automated content pipeline from ideation to distribution. Research, draft, edit, format, publish -- orchestrated across agents with quality gates at every step.

**Persistent Memory**
Shared context that persists across sessions and agents. Your AI team remembers past decisions, ongoing projects, and established preferences. No more re-explaining your business every time.

## SECTION 4: HOW IT WORKS

**Section Label:**
How it works

**Headline:**
Three steps to a coordinated AI workforce.

**Step 1**
**Connect Your AIs**
Bring your existing API keys for Claude, Gemini, Codex, and Perplexity. Hive Mind does not replace your AI subscriptions -- it orchestrates them. Your models, your accounts, your data.

**Step 2**
**Define Your Workflows**
Set up routing rules for your business. Marketing prompts go to your content team. Engineering tasks hit your dev stack. Strategy questions convene the full war room. Configure once, run forever.

**Step 3**
**Let the Hive Work**
Issue a single command. Perplexity scouts real-time intel first. The router dispatches to the right agents. They run in parallel, share context, and converge results. You review and approve from one dashboard.

**Visual Note:**
Horizontal 3-step flow with numbered circles (1, 2, 3) in violet. Connecting lines between steps. Dark background. Each step has a short title above and description below.

- Smooth scroll between sections
- Waitlist form: inline validation, success state replaces form with confirmation text

**Responsive:**
- Feature grid collapses to single column on mobile
- Hero headline scales down but stays bold
- Cards stack vertically with 16px gap on mobile
