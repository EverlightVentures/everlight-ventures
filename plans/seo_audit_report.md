The current Sam titles follow the pattern: "Sam's [Ordinal] Superpower" with subtitle "Adventures with Sam and Robo, Book [N]."

**Strengths:**
- Clear series branding
- Ordinal numbers create natural reading order
- Subtitle includes series name (helps Amazon link to series page)

**Weaknesses:**
- No keyword in the title itself (no "phonics," "coloring book," "STEM," etc.)

### 7.2 Ebook Pricing Analysis: $5.99

**Verdict: APPROVED with one caveat.**

- At $5.99, you qualify for KDP's 70% royalty rate (available for $2.99-$9.99). Royalty per ebook: approximately $4.19 (before delivery cost).
- At $2.99, royalty was approximately $2.09. The $5.99 price point doubles your per-unit revenue.
- Comparable interactive/educational children's ebooks on Amazon range from $3.99-$7.99. $5.99 is the sweet spot -- not cheap enough to signal low quality, not expensive enough to create purchase hesitation.
- **Caveat:** Consider a $3.99 launch price for the first 30 days of each book to build rank and reviews, then raise to $5.99. Amazon's algorithm rewards sales velocity during the launch window.

**Series bundle ebook pricing:**
- Sam Box Set (5 books): $19.99 (33% savings vs. $29.95 individual)
- Luna Box Set (5 books): $19.99
- Trilogy Set (3 books): $12.99 (28% savings vs. $17.97 individual)
- Complete Universe (13 main + 2 grief): Consider $49.99 mega-bundle

### 7.3 Paperback Pricing Analysis: $10.99

**Verdict: APPROVED.**

- 8.5x8.5 full-color interior paperbacks on KDP have a base printing cost of approximately $5.50-$7.00 depending on page count (48-56 pages).
- At $10.99 with a $6.50 average print cost, KDP royalty is approximately $1.75-$2.25 per unit (60% royalty on paperback).
- This is tight but viable. The margin is thin for ads -- you will need to rely on organic search and series sell-through rather than high-cost PPC for paperbacks.
- **Competitive analysis:** Similar format (interactive coloring + story, 8.5x8.5, full color) competitors price at $9.99-$14.99. You are at the value end, which is correct for a new brand building volume.

### 7.4 Audiobook Pricing Analysis: $3.99

**Verdict: APPROVED with distribution notes.**

- If distributing through ACX (Audible), you do not control pricing. Audible sets the price and pays royalties based on their tiers. $3.99 is only achievable through direct distribution (your own website, Google Play, Apple Books, or services like Findaway Voices/Authors Direct).
- For ACX/Audible: expect Audible to price at $3.95-$6.95 for children's audiobooks under 1 hour. Your royalty will be 25% (non-exclusive) or 40% (exclusive) of the sale price.
- **Recommendation:** Use Findaway Voices for wide distribution (Apple, Google, Kobo, Scribd, libraries) at your chosen $3.99 price point. List on Audible as well but accept their pricing model.
- Children's audiobooks benefit enormously from professional narration with character voices. Budget $200-$400 per book for a professional narrator (PFH rate of $150-$300 for a 15-20 minute children's audiobook).

### 7.5 Hardcover Pricing (Missing from Target)

The current metadata lists hardcovers at $19.99 via IngramSpark. Your target pricing excludes hardcovers.

**Recommendation:** Keep hardcovers at $19.99-$22.99 via IngramSpark for the library and gift market. Libraries strongly prefer hardcovers. Hardcovers also signal premium quality and justify the series as a "gift set" purchase. This adds 15 more SKUs (60 total products) and opens the institutional sales channel.

## SECTION 8: KDP-COMPLIANT DESCRIPTION TEMPLATE

### 8.1 HTML Tag Compliance

**CRITICAL FIX NEEDED:** The current Sam book descriptions use `<ul>` and `<li>` tags. These are NOT supported by KDP. Amazon will either strip them (creating a wall of unformatted text) or reject the listing.

**KDP-supported HTML tags only:**
- `<b>` -- bold
- `<i>` -- italic
- `<br>` -- line break
- `<p>` -- paragraph
- `<h1>` through `<h6>` -- headings (use sparingly; they render large)

No `<ul>`, `<li>`, `<a>`, `<img>`, `<div>`, `<span>`, or any other tags.

### 8.2 Description Template (Under 4000 Characters)

This template works for all 15 books. Customize the bracketed sections per book.

