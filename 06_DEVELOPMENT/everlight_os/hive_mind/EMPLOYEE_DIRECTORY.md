# Everlight AI Hive Mind -- Employee Directory
> 4 Departments. 63 Named Employees. 1 Mission: $10k/mo and beyond.

---

## How This Team Works

- **No solo missions.** Every task activates employees across departments. A blog post involves Content Strategy (Claude) + Writer (Codex) + Distribution Ops (Gemini) + Tech & AI beat (Perplexity) minimum.
- **Perplexity's newsroom always runs first** -- fresh intel before anyone moves.
- **Cross-department Slack threads** are the norm. Every deliverable has at least 2 departments in the thread.
- **The War Room** (`_logs/ai_war_room/`) is where big decisions get debated. All 4 department heads weigh in.
- **Intel-first protocol:** Perplexity Intel delivers overnight digest at 5 AM PT. No department acts on stale information.
- **Minimum staffing per task:** 3 employees across 2+ departments. No exceptions.
