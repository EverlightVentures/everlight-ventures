What This Shows

Telemetry before storytelling.

The watchtower is designed to show whether the system is fit to act. Fresh data beats loud claims. If the feed is stale, the board says it. If the pulse is dangerous, the board says it. If the last trade closed badly, the board says that too.

Deterministic controls stay binding

Freshness gates, hard drawdown protections, pulse danger blocks, and risk checks are not optional marketing copy.

Supabase is the public proof layer

Oracle exports metrics, feature snapshots, and trade labels into tables the site and Business OS can read.

The board is one machine

Hive, n8n, Stripe, broker workflows, and the trading watchtower all roll up into the same operating view.
