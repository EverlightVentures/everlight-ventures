## SECTION 6: FAQ

**Section Header:** Questions? Straight answers.

### Q1: How does Onyx POS pricing compare to Square or Toast?
Square charges 2.6% + $0.10 per transaction. On $30,000 in monthly sales, that is roughly $780 in processing fees alone -- and that is before their software fees. Toast charges even more with long-term contracts and hardware requirements. Onyx is a flat $49/month with competitive processing rates that do not scale against your revenue. The more you sell, the more you save.

### Q2: What hardware do I need?
Any modern tablet (iPad or Android), a phone, or a computer with a browser. Onyx is fully web-based. If you want a dedicated setup, we support standard receipt printers, barcode scanners, and card readers -- but none of it is required to get started.

### Q3: Can I migrate my data from my current POS system?
Yes. We support CSV imports for product catalogs, customer lists, and historical sales data. If you are migrating from Square, Clover, or Shopify POS, we have one-click import templates that handle the formatting for you.

### Q4: Is there a contract or cancellation fee?
No. Onyx is month-to-month. You can cancel from your dashboard at any time with zero fees, zero phone calls, and zero retention specialists trying to talk you out of it.

### Q5: What kind of support do I get?
Email and live chat support from real people, not chatbots. Our support team responds within 2 hours during business hours and within 8 hours on evenings and weekends. We also maintain a full knowledge base with video walkthroughs for every feature.

## DESIGN NOTES FOR LOVABLE

**Color Palette:**
- Background: Dark (#0A0A0A or #111111)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) with muted gray (#A1A1A1) for secondary text
- Accent: Gold/Amber (#D4A017 or #F59E0B) for CTAs, highlights, and the Onyx wordmark
- Cards: Slightly elevated dark (#1A1A1A) with subtle border (#2A2A2A)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, large, tight letter-spacing (think Inter or Satoshi)
- Body: Clean, readable, generous line-height
- Product name "ONYX" should feel heavy and premium

**Layout:**
- Full-width sections with generous vertical padding
- Features grid: 3 columns on desktop, stacked on mobile
- Pricing card: centered, single card, gold border or accent
- Testimonials: card layout with subtle background differentiation
- Final CTA: full-width dark section with centered email input and button

**Animations (subtle):**
- Fade-in on scroll for each section
- Slight scale on hover for CTA buttons
- Feature cards with soft hover elevation

**Framework:** PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve) -- the copy flows from pain (Section 2) through features as the solution (Section 3) to the close (Sections 4-7).
