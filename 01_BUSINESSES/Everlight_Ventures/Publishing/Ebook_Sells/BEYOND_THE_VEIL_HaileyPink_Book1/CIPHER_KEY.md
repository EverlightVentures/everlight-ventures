### Punctuation

Spaces between words are marked with a centered dot: **.**

No other punctuation is encoded. Periods, commas, and apostrophes from the original messages are omitted in the cipher text. The words themselves carry the meaning.

3. Work left to right, translating each symbol using the key above.
