## 5. Customer Acquisition Strategy

### ONYX POS -- Getting to 30+ Customers

**Target ICP**: Small retail businesses with 1-5 employees, no existing POS or frustrated with Square/Toast pricing. Nurseries, boutiques, auto parts, smoke shops, pet stores, specialty food.

**Channel Priority**:

| Channel | CAC Estimate | Volume | Timeline |
|---------|-------------|--------|----------|
| Cold outreach (local biz) | $0 (time only) | 2-5 customers/mo | Month 1+ |
| Google Ads ("cheap POS system") | $80-200/customer | 3-8 customers/mo | Month 2+ |
| Product Hunt launch | $0 | 5-15 trial signups (burst) | Month 2 |
| Local business groups/chambers | $0-50 | 1-3 customers/mo | Month 2+ |
| Referral program ($50 credit) | $50/customer | 1-3 customers/mo | Month 3+ |
| G2/Capterra listings | $0 | 1-2 customers/mo (organic) | Month 3+ |
| Partnership: Shopify migration | $0 | 1-2 customers/mo | Month 4+ |

**Cold Outreach Script** (for nurseries -- your proof case):

> "Hey [Name], I built a POS system for a local nursery -- handles sales, time clock, inventory, payroll, everything -- for $49/month flat. No per-transaction fees, no hardware to buy. You can try it free for 14 days. Want me to show you a 2-minute demo?"

**Key proof points**: Mountain Gardens Nursery ran it from Nov 2025 to present. Real sales data, real employee time tracking. That is your case study.

**Action items**:
1. Record a 2-minute screen capture of MGN running (this week)
2. Build a list of 50 nurseries, boutiques, and auto shops in your region
3. Send 10 cold emails per day using the script above
4. Set up a Google Ads campaign targeting "affordable POS system small business" ($10-20/day budget)

One of Everlight Ventures' biggest advantages is the portfolio. Every customer touchpoint is a chance to funnel into another product.

### Cross-Sell Matrix

| HIM Loadout | Hive Mind waitlist | Blog sidebar: "Automate your side hustle with Hive Mind" |
| Logistics client | Onyx POS | Direct pitch: "Track your retail sales with our POS" |

### Unified Email List Strategy

All products feed into ONE master email list (segmented by interest):
- Segment: SaaS buyers (Onyx, Hive Mind)
- Segment: Readers (kids, adult fiction)
- Segment: Entrepreneurs (HIM Loadout, Hive Mind)
- Segment: Business services (Logistics)

Tool: ConvertKit free tier (up to 1,000 subscribers) or Mailchimp free tier.

Monthly newsletter goes to all segments with section blocks relevant to each. Cross-pollination happens naturally.

## Summary: The Path to $10K/Month

1. **Onyx POS is the engine.** It is 3-6 weeks from generating revenue, has a proven user, and SaaS MRR compounds. Ship it first. Everything else is secondary until Onyx has 5 paying customers.

2. **Publishing is the floor.** It generates revenue while you sleep. The immediate action is optimizing existing Amazon listings (free, zero effort) and launching Beyond the Veil with an audiobook (highest margin format).

3. **Hive Mind is the ceiling.** Higher TAM, higher price potential, but needs product-market fit validation. Do not spend significant time on it until month 3-4. Build the waitlist with content in the meantime.

4. **Logistics pays bills.** If you can close 1-2 fulfillment clients in month 2-3, that cash flow funds everything else.

5. **HIM Loadout is a slow burn.** Write the SEO content now, collect affiliate revenue in 3-6 months when Google indexes it.

6. **Alley Kingz and XLM Bot SaaS are distractions right now.** Do not touch them until you have $5K/month from other streams.

7. **$10K/month is realistic by month 8-12.** The aggressive scenario gets close by month 6. The conservative scenario hits it by month 10-12. The key variable is Onyx POS customer acquisition velocity.

**Single most important thing to do this week**: Finish Onyx POS multi-tenant isolation and record the MGN demo video. That unlocks everything.
