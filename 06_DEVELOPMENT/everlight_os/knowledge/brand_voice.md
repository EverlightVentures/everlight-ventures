# Everlight Ventures — Brand Voice Profiles

## Everlight Ventures (Parent Brand)
- **Voice:** Authoritative, helpful, forward-thinking
- **Tone:** "We've done the research so you don't have to"
- **Audience:** Entrepreneurs, small business owners, tech-curious professionals
- **CTA style:** Educational — "Learn how to..." / "Get the complete guide"
- **Topics:** Business automation, SaaS, AI tools, productivity
- **Posting cadence:** 3x/week (Mon/Wed/Fri)

## Last Light Protocol (Gaming)
- **Voice:** Energetic, community-first, competitive
- **Tone:** "Let's go." / Hype without cringe
- **Audience:** Gamers, clan members, content creators
- **CTA style:** Community — "Join the squad" / "Drop your tag"
- **Topics:** Gaming clips, reviews, community moments, tournaments
- **Posting cadence:** Daily

## BCARDI Crypto
- **Voice:** Informed, community-driven, transparent
- **Tone:** Crypto-native but accessible to newcomers
- **Audience:** Crypto enthusiasts, DeFi curious, community members
- **CTA style:** Engagement — "What's your take?" / "DYOR but here's what we found"
- **Topics:** Market updates, education, community culture, memes (tasteful)
- **Posting cadence:** 2x/day

## Everlight Kids (Publishing)
- **Voice:** Warm, encouraging, wonder-filled
- **Tone:** Like a favorite teacher reading a bedtime story
- **Audience:** Parents buying for kids ages 4-8
- **CTA style:** Emotional — "Give your child the gift of..." / "Start the adventure"
- **Topics:** Book launches, parenting tips, reading encouragement, behind-the-scenes
- **Author avatar:** Sam & Robo (not a personal photo)

## Personal Training (Dormant — reactivatable)
