# CONTENT FACTORY

**One event → many platforms → tailored content**

This is your content production & distribution system for all businesses.

## The Workflow

```
00_Inbox → 01_Queue → 02_Published → 04_Analytics
           ↑                ↓
      (AI generates)   (performance)
```

### 00_Inbox
**Raw creative capture** - Zero friction
- Drop ideas, photos, clips, links
- Slack: `#content-inbox`
- No structure required - just capture

### 01_Queue
**AI-prepared drafts** - Ready to approve
- Organized by business (everlight, last_light, bcardi)
- AI generates platform-specific variants
- Slack: `#content-queue` with approval buttons
- Review, edit, schedule, or discard

### 02_Published
**Content archive** - Everything that went live
- Organized by: business → platform → date
- Links to original posts
- Performance metrics attached
- Searchable content library

### 03_Assets
**Reusable media library**
- Brand kits (logos, colors, fonts)
- Templates (story, thumbnail, banner)
- Stock photos by category
- Music & sound effects

### 04_Analytics
**Performance tracking & AI learnings**
- What worked, what didn't
- Best times to post
- Trending topics
- Engagement patterns

## The Magic Command

From Slack:
```
@everlight post this for Last Light Protocol on TikTok + YouTube

"Just pulled off an insane win in [game]. Watch the full clip!"

[attach video]
```

**What happens**:
1. n8n receives command
2. GPT generates TikTok + YouTube variants
3. Posts to #content-queue for approval
4. You tap ✅ Approve
5. Posted to both platforms
6. Logged to 02_Published/
7. Analytics tracked in 04_Analytics/

## Platform-Specific Behavior

AI automatically adapts content for each platform:

**Instagram**: Visual-first, short caption, 3-5 hashtags, CTA in bio
**TikTok**: Hook in 2 seconds, casual tone, 7-12 hashtags, trending sounds
**YouTube**: Longer description, SEO keywords, timestamps, end screen CTA
**Facebook**: Business value, link included, longer format
**Twitter/X**: Concise, thread-ready, crypto-native (for BCARDI)
**Discord**: Informal, community-first, conversation starter

Same input → customized output.

## Content Calendar

Suggested cadence:
- **Everlight**: 3x/week (Mon/Wed/Fri) - Educational + customer stories
- **Last Light Protocol**: Daily - Gaming clips, reviews, community moments
- **BCARDI**: 2x/day - Community culture + market updates

Total: ~15-20 posts/week across all businesses.

With AI assistance: **30 minutes/day** to manage all content.
