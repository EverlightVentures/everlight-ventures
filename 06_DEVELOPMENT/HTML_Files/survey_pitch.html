### Survey

Introduction:

  • "Hey, it's me! I'm in the neighborhood doing a quick survey. I noticed your car and wanted to ask a few questions. Do you happen to have an electric car?"
    • If yes: "That's awesome! What do you like or dislike about it?"
    • If no: "Gotcha, no worries! Just curious about how many people around here are going electric."

Solar Question:

  • "Next question, do you have solar panels on your roof?"
    • If yes: "Great! How's it working out for you? Any challenges?"
    • If no: "Gotcha, no problem. A lot of folks around here have been getting offers for solar, though. Was that something you considered but didn’t move forward with?"

PG&E Question:

  • "Okay, last one for now: How do you feel about PG&E and their rates?"
    • Most will express frustration with PG&E rates.
    • "Yeah, a lot of people are frustrated with those rates. I totally get it. Are you concerned about the wildfire risk with them too?"
    • Agree and empathize.

The Reason for the Survey:

  • "So the reason I'm asking these questions is that there's been an update to the IRA (Inflation Reduction Act). You pay taxes, right? Well, whether you wanted to or not, you’ve been contributing to a fund for solar and battery benefits."
  • "Essentially, qualifying homeowners can get their tax money back in the form of solar and batteries installed on their home."
  • "In return, you set up a relationship with us as your power company, and we can help reduce your energy bill by up to 40% by eliminating distribution costs and unrelated fees."
  • "We monitor and maintain the equipment, and when you sell your house, the contract transfers just like a PG&E bill."

Qualification Check:

  • "Obviously, if you qualify, it’s a no-brainer, right?"
  • "The only thing is, not everyone qualifies. For example, Mary down the road really wanted to, but she wasn’t on the title. Are you on the title here?"
    • If yes: "Awesome! That’s great to hear. Also, is the roof under 20 years old?"
    • If yes: "Perfect! And do you typically use around 500kWh or about $150 worth of energy a month?"

Final Qualification:

  • "Great! Last question: Is your credit score average or above average?"
    • If yes: "Awesome, we’re almost there!"

Next Steps:

  • "My job is really easy – I just need to pass this information to my engineer, and he’ll create a custom savings report for you."
  • "When you switch from a monopoly to a competitive energy market, you get better rates. We’ll pull 4 custom reports to see who can save you the most."
  • "He’s in the area today and tomorrow. What time works best for you? This evening or tomorrow?"

Gratitude & Closing:

  • "Awesome, [Name], I’ll see you on [date and time]. And my engineer doesn’t like to show up empty-handed. Whether we continue or not, we like to show our appreciation for your time. Do you prefer wine or chocolates?"
  • "Thanks again for your time, and I’ll see you soon!"
