Sam has a habit of noticing things.

So when he and his robot friend Robo walk their favorite riverside path one Saturday morning and notice the river is sick -- trash on the banks, murky water, a patient heron waiting for the water to be clean again -- Sam cannot walk past it.

With a handwritten list from his elderly neighbor Mr. Adeyemi and Robo by his side, Sam goes door to door, makes flyers full of long-vowel words, and organizes a neighborhood cleanup. Only four people show up. But four people can make a start.
