## Integration Instructions

Each footer should be placed at the very end of its chapter, formatted as follows in the manuscript:

```

                              . . .

!.~/.%#]#.!.\#>#].:#@}

```

The three centered dots act as a visual breath before the cipher line.
The cipher line itself should be centered, set in a monospace or decorative font if available, at a slightly smaller font size than body text.

On Kindle, use HTML centering in the .epub/.mobi:
```html
<p style="text-align:center; font-size:0.85em; letter-spacing:0.15em; margin-top:2em; color:#666;">
. . .
</p>
<p style="text-align:center; font-family:monospace; font-size:0.9em; letter-spacing:0.1em; margin-top:0.5em;">
!.~/.%#]#.!.\#>#].:#@}
</p>
```

## The Hidden Narrative Arc

Read in sequence, the decoded messages tell a complete story:

| Chapter | Message | Emotional Beat |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Prologue | I am here I never left | Presence -- announcing |
| Ch1 | The dead do not leave We just change frequency | Explaining -- echoes the dedication |
| Ch2 | I can see the threads They connect everything | Wonder -- seeing the web |
| Ch3 | The one you call the Drift is in pain He does not know he is hurting you | Compassion -- understanding the enemy |
| Ch4 | She is stronger than she knows Tell her I said that | Pride -- big brother watching |
| Ch5 | The web remembers every act of love None of it is lost | Comfort -- nothing is wasted |
| Ch6 | She is flying now She is so close I can almost reach her | Excitement -- she is near |
| Ch7 | The sacrifice is not death It is crossing I know because I crossed | Revelation -- he knows the truth |
| Ch8 | I am proud of her I am proud of my sister | Love -- the emotional peak |
| Ch9 | We are all threads in the same web Separation is the only illusion | Wisdom -- the cosmic truth |
| Ch10 | Find me in the hum I will be waiting I love you | Farewell -- but not goodbye |

The voice moves from quiet observation to direct emotional address. By Ch8, the reader knows who is speaking. By Ch10, the voice breaks the fourth wall -- speaking not just to Hailey, but to the reader, and to the author.
