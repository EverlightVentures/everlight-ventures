# DEDICATION PAGE
## Beyond the Veil -- Suggested Dedication Copy

---

## OPTION A (Simple and Powerful)

For my brother.

You crossed beyond the veil on September 3, 2024.
I wrote this book to follow you there.

Fly free.

---

## OPTION B (More Personal)

This book is dedicated to my brother,
who left this world on September 3, 2024.

You always said the dead do not really leave.
They just go somewhere we cannot follow yet.

I believe you now.

This is me finding my way to where you are.

---

## OPTION C (Woven into the Story's Theme)

For my brother.

Somewhere in the quantum web, across every timeline and dimension,
you are still flying.

September 3, 2024 -- the day you crossed beyond the veil.
This story is the door I built to find you on the other side.

---

## RECOMMENDATION

Option B. It is personal without being heavy. It echoes the book's themes
(the dead do not leave / just go somewhere we cannot follow yet) in a way
that will resonate even for readers who do not know your story. And it ends
on love, which is what dedication pages are for.

---

## PLACEMENT NOTE

The dedication page goes:
  - After the title page
  - Before the table of contents
  - Before Chapter 1

It should be its own page, centered, with generous white space above and
below the text. Simple. Let it breathe.
