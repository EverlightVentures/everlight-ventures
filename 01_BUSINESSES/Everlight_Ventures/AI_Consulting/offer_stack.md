# Everlight AI Consulting -- Offer Stack

## Positioning
"Meta uses 42 AI agents to run their company. We built the same thing for SMBs."

## Offer Tiers

### Tier 1: Discovery Session ($1)
- 30-min live call with AI demo
- Show prospect their business through AI lens
- Identify 3 automation opportunities
- CTA: Book via Stripe Checkout on /funnel/consulting/

### Tier 2: AI Agent Build ($2,000 - $5,000)
- Custom n8n workflow deployment
- 1-3 AI agents (lead gen, support, appointment setter)
- Integration with their existing tools (CRM, email, calendar)
- 2-week delivery
- Includes 30 days of support

### Tier 3: Monthly Retainer ($2,000/mo)
- Ongoing AI agent management
- Performance monitoring + optimization
- New agent development as needed
- Priority support
- Monthly ROI report

## Target Verticals (Priority Order)
1. Dental practices (high ticket, tech-friendly, appointment-heavy)
2. Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical -- lead gen pain)
3. Digital agencies under 50 employees (understand AI, need implementation)
4. Legal practices (document processing, intake automation)
5. Real estate teams (lead follow-up, CRM automation)

## Sales Process
1. Prospect scraper finds SMBs via Google Maps API
2. Lead scorer rates by AI-readiness signals
3. Piper Reeves drafts warm outreach (7-touch, 21 days)
4. Discovery call ($1 Stripe checkout)
5. Proposal generated from intake form
6. Build phase (2 weeks)
7. Retainer upsell at delivery

## Key Differentiators
- We run 42 AI agents across 8 businesses (proof of concept)
- We built a POS system, trading bot, broker platform, publishing engine -- all AI-powered
- Not just chatbots -- full agentic workflows that replace manual processes
- California-based, not offshore

## Revenue Target
- 5 clients/month at $3k build + $2k retainer = $25k/mo within 6 months
- Fits the $10k/mo Everlight target as the highest-margin line
