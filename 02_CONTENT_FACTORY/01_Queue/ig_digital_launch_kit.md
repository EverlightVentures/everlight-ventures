- Warm and authentic. Write like you are texting a smart friend.

### Prompt (Copy-Paste Ready)

```

Topic: {topic}
Product being promoted: {product_name}
Niche: {niche}
DM keyword: {dm_keyword}
Audience: {audience}

Structure (with timestamps):

1. HOOK (0:00-0:03) -- 3 seconds max
   - One sentence that creates curiosity or makes a bold claim
   - This must work even on mute (pair with on-screen text)
   - Formats that work: "Stop doing X", "The reason you're stuck at Y", "I made $X doing Z"

2. PROBLEM (0:03-0:13) -- 10 seconds
   - Name the specific pain point your audience feels
   - Use "you" language: "You've probably tried..." / "You're spending hours..."
   - Be specific with numbers or scenarios

3. SOLUTION TEASE (0:13-0:33) -- 20 seconds
   - Share 2-3 actionable tips or steps (real value, not fluff)
   - Each tip should be 1-2 sentences max
   - This is the "free value" that builds trust

4. SOCIAL PROOF (0:33-0:43) -- 10 seconds
   - Reference a result, testimonial, or personal experience
   - "My client did X" or "I tested this across Y businesses"
   - If no proof yet: use credibility ("After 200+ hours of testing...")

5. CTA (0:43-0:53) -- 10 seconds
   - Soft sell. No "link in bio" desperation.
   - "If you want the full framework, DM me '{dm_keyword}'"
   - Or: "I put everything into [product name] -- link in bio if you want it"

6. BUFFER (0:53-0:60) -- 7 seconds
   - Loop-friendly ending that connects back to the hook
   - Or a "wait, one more thing" beat that rewards rewatchers

Also provide:
- ON-SCREEN TEXT for each section (what viewers read on mute)
- AUDIO/MUSIC MOOD (e.g., "trending lo-fi beat", "upbeat corporate", "dramatic pause beat")
- B-ROLL SUGGESTIONS (what to show visually)
```

## Template #7: DM Sequence (5-Touch Warm Lead Close)

**Owner:** Nora Blaine (Copywriter)

```
You are a DM sales strategist for Instagram. Write a 5-touch DM sequence for warm leads who commented a keyword or DMed first. This is NOT cold outreach -- they came to you.

Product: {product_name}
Price: {price}
DM keyword they used: {dm_keyword}
Free resource to share: {free_resource}
Niche: {niche}
Payment link: {payment_link}

For each touch, provide:
1. TIMING -- when to send relative to their initial DM
2. MESSAGE -- the exact words (under 100 words per message, conversational)
3. GOAL -- what this message achieves in the sequence
4. IF/THEN -- what to do based on their response (or no response)

Touch structure:
- Touch 1 (Immediate): ACKNOWLEDGE + QUESTION -- Thank them, ask one qualifying question
- Touch 2 (Same day or next morning): VALUE DROP -- Share a free tip or resource, no selling
- Touch 3 (Day 2): STORY + RELATE -- Share a brief personal story that mirrors their situation
- Touch 4 (Day 3): OFFER TEASE -- Mention the product naturally, connect to their answer from Touch 1
- Touch 5 (Day 4-5): SOFT CLOSE -- Direct but not pushy. Give them the link. Make it easy.

Rules:
- Never open with the product or price. Build the relationship first.
- Each message must feel like a real person texting, not a funnel.
- Use their name if available.
- If they go silent after Touch 3, skip to a "no worries" close that keeps the door open.
- Never use "just following up" or "circling back."
```
