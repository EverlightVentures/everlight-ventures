# Sam & Robo — Series Bible

## Series Overview
**"Adventures with Sam"** is a children's picture book series about a curious, brave kid named Sam who discovers superpowers — each one connected to emotional intelligence and real-life skills. Sam is accompanied by Robo, a loyal robot companion who provides comic relief, asks the questions kids are thinking, and helps Sam learn.

**Mission:** Teach kids ages 4-8 that their real superpowers are emotional skills — kindness, patience, courage, empathy, perseverance.

---

## Characters

### Sam
- **Age:** Around 6-7 years old
- **Personality:** Curious, brave, kind-hearted, sometimes impulsive
- **Appearance:** Bright eyes, energetic posture, casual kid clothes. Expressive face that shows emotions clearly.
- **Speech:** Simple, enthusiastic sentences. Uses exclamation marks. Asks "why?" and "how?" a lot.
- **Arc:** Each book, Sam discovers a new "superpower" (emotional skill) through an adventure
- **Quirk:** Gets excited and wants to help everyone, sometimes before understanding the situation

### Robo
- **Personality:** Loyal, helpful, slightly literal-minded, warm despite being a robot
- **Appearance:** Friendly, round robot design. Not scary or militaristic. Big expressive eyes/screen.
- **Speech:** Slightly formal/quirky but warm. Says things like "My sensors indicate..." or "Processing... yes, that sounds like a good plan, Sam!"
- **Role:** Comic relief + question-asker. Asks the "dumb questions" that help explain concepts to the reader.
- **Quirk:** Sometimes takes things too literally, which creates funny moments

### Luna (Sam & Luna series — newer)
- **Personality:** Calm, observant, creative. Complements Sam's energy.
- **Appearance:** Thoughtful expression, artistic clothing/accessories
- **Role:** New friend who brings different strengths. Shows kids that different approaches are valuable.

## Visual Style Guide

### Cover Art
- **Style:** Vibrant, colorful, kid-friendly digital art
- **Characters:** Expressive faces, dynamic poses, clearly recognizable
- **Color palette:** Bright primaries + warm secondary colors. No dark/scary palettes.
- **Composition:** Sam and Robo front and center, adventure scene in background
- **Title placement:** Top third, bold readable font
- **Series branding:** "Adventures with Sam" banner consistent across all covers

### Interior Illustrations
- **Style:** Full-page illustrations facing text pages (spread format)
- **Detail level:** Rich enough to engage, simple enough to not overwhelm
- **Backgrounds:** Varied settings (home, school, outdoors, fantasy) per story
- **Emotional range:** Illustrations must match the emotional beat (joy, concern, triumph, tenderness)

### Coloring Pages
- **Style:** Black and white line art
- **Lines:** Thick, clean outlines — easy for ages 4-8 to color
- **Complexity:** Simple to medium. Large areas to color, not tiny details.
- **Content:** Character portraits, key scenes, activity pages

## Story Structure Template

Every Sam book follows this arc:

1. **Opening** (pages 1-4): Sam and Robo in their normal world. Something catches Sam's attention.
2. **Call to adventure** (pages 5-8): A problem or challenge presents itself. Sam wants to help.
3. **Attempt & struggle** (pages 9-14): Sam tries but hits obstacles. Learns what doesn't work.
4. **Discovery** (pages 15-18): Sam discovers the "superpower" (emotional skill) through experience.
5. **Triumph** (pages 19-22): Sam uses the new skill to solve the problem.
6. **Reflection** (pages 23-24): Sam and Robo talk about what they learned. Warm ending.

**Robo's role throughout:** Asks questions, tries to help (sometimes clumsily), celebrates with Sam.

## Continuity Rules

- Sam's appearance stays consistent across all books
- Robo's design stays consistent across all books
- Each book should have a subtle callback to a previous superpower
- The superpowers build on each other — Sam doesn't forget previous ones
- Author display name: "Everlight Kids" (not a personal name)
- Author avatar: Sam & Robo illustration (not a personal photo)
