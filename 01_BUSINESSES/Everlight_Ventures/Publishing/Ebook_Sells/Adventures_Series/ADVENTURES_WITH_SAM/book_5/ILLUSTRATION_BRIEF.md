## FORMAT REQUIREMENT -- CRITICAL

Every scene requires **TWO illustrations:**

1. **B&W Coloring Page:** Professional children's coloring book line art. Thick bold clean black outlines on pure white background. No color whatsoever. Gentle grey shading for depth and dimension. Open white space for kids to color with crayons/markers. NOT flat -- use grey tones to guide the child.

2. **Full Color Illustration:** Same scene, same angle, fully colored. 3D digital animation style, high-quality Disney/Pixar aesthetic, cinematic lighting, vibrant saturated colors. Rich detail.

Both versions show the SAME scene from the SAME angle. The B&W version is the line art of the color page.

**Total:** 24 scene illustrations (12 color + 12 B&W) + 1 cover = **25 images**

## ART STYLE -- MATCH BOOKS 1-4

- **Genre:** 3D digital animation, Disney/Pixar aesthetic, children's picture book
- **Rendering:** High-quality 3D with cinematic lighting, volumetric light, soft shadows
- **Faces:** Large expressive eyes, simple noses, wide mouths that show emotion clearly
- **Colors (full color):** Highly saturated, vibrant, warm. Greens are GREEN. Blues are BLUE. Rich and alive.
- **Backgrounds:** Detailed but not cluttered. Every element identifiable. Nature scenes with individual flowers, leaves, rocks, clouds a child can point to and name.
- **B&W pages:** Thick bold clean black outlines on white background. Grey shading for depth. No color. Professional coloring book quality.

## CHARACTER REFERENCE -- CANONICAL SPECS

### SAM
- **Age:** 6-7 years old (oldest in the series -- looks slightly more mature and confident)
- **Hair:** Messy, spiky brown hair (outside-kid hair, not styled)
- **Eyes:** Large, expressive brown eyes -- the first thing you notice
- **Face:** Round, warm, animated. Energetic posture.
- **Skin:** Warm, sun-touched
- **Outfit:** Red t-shirt (series standard), jeans, scuffed sneakers
- **Backpack:** Slightly too big for him, worn, patched in one corner, always present
- **Demeanor:** Confident, kind, leader energy -- but still a kid

### ROBO
- **Size:** Compact, Sam's height or slightly shorter
- **Body:** Rounded silver/white metallic, friendly, non-threatening
- **Head:** Rounded with small antenna (ball on stick)
- **Eyes:** Big glowing blue LED circles. No mouth. Eyes change shape for emotion (wide = curious, curved up = happy, angled = concern).
- **Chest Display:** Rectangular screen panel showing icons and simple graphics. PRIMARY EMOTIONAL INDICATOR. Must be legible in every scene.
- **Limbs:** Simple, rounded. Feet are flat/boot-like.

### MS. REYES (NEW)
- **Build:** Tall, professional posture
- **Face:** Kind eyes, warm and competent
- **Outfit:** Professional but approachable -- blouse, slacks, always carries a clipboard
- **Role:** Community center director. Appears Scenes 5_2, 5_7, 5_10.

### MIA (NEW -- age ~5-6)
- **Hair:** Two braids
- **Hands:** Paint stains on fingers (always)
- **Face:** Creative sparkle in eyes, quick to smile
- **Vibe:** Artistic, expressive, joyful

### OLIVER (NEW -- age ~5-6)
- **Hair:** Neat, tidy
- **Glasses:** Round frames, pushes them up frequently
- **Face:** Studious, serious when thinking, bright when excited
- **Vibe:** Engineering mind, meticulous, quietly proud

### DANNY (NEW -- age ~5-6)
- **Build:** Smallest of the group
- **Face:** Gentle, quiet, soft expression
- **Demeanor:** Still and patient. Animals gravitate to him.
- **Vibe:** The gentle soul of the group

### PRIYA (NEW -- age ~5-6)
- **Hair:** Dark, possibly in a ponytail or loose
- **Eyes:** Curious, bright, always observing
- **Prop:** Carries a journal/notebook
- **Vibe:** Scientist. Asks questions about everything.

### JAX (NEW -- age ~5-6)
- **Build:** Slightly tougher exterior than the others
- **Posture:** Arms often crossed (early scenes), gradually opens up
- **Face:** Guarded at first, then vulnerable, then proud
- **Arc:** Goes from "reading is boring" to reading his first book out loud. His transformation is the emotional spine of the book.

### LILY (returning from earlier books)
- **Hair:** Short, dark, sometimes in a ponytail
- **Eyes:** Large, bright, friendly
- **Outfit:** Casual -- overalls or t-shirt. Energetic kid.
- **Role:** Sam's friend. Appears Scenes 5_5, 5_6, 5_10.

### GRANDMA (NEW -- most important new character)
- **Height:** Shorter than expected
- **Hair:** Silver-white, pulled back in a braid
- **Eyes:** Dark, warm, bright -- THE SAME EYES AS SAM. This visual connection is critical.
- **Face:** When she looks at Sam, her whole body turns toward him. Deep love visible.
- **Outfit:** Simple blue dress
- **Prop:** Carries a basket covered with cloth (contains the Book of Superpowers)
- **Appears:** Scenes 5_11, 5_12 only. Her arrival is the emotional climax.

### RETURNING CHARACTERS (Scene 5_10 celebration)
- **Mr. Green:** 50s-60s, recycling center cap, work apron, kind face
- **Farmer Brown:** 50s-60s, wide-brimmed hat, broad-shouldered, flannel
- **Ranger Jane:** 40s, ranger uniform/hat, athletic, capable
- **Dr. Spark:** Lab coat (always stained), bright excited eyes
