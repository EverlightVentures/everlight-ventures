### Book Description (Blurb)

She escapes her bruises every night by leaving her body behind.

Deputy Hailey Pink is trapped in a dying Western town where the sheriff dismisses her warnings, and the man waiting for her at home speaks with his fists before his words. But when Hailey closes her eyes and sleeps, she flies. In the astral realm she is untouchable -- soaring over floating islands of liquid light, swimming ancient oceans alongside creatures older than memory. She is powerful in ways this world will never allow her to be.

But something is following her there. A shadow. A presence. A dark and ancient rage that does not belong to this world, or any other she has ever known. When the people of her town begin drifting through the streets like hollow shells -- a sickness no doctor can name, a silence that feels like something feeding -- Hailey starts to understand. The astral realm is not a dream. It is real. The worlds are connected. And whatever is hunting her in the dark is bleeding into her waking life, destroying everyone she has sworn to protect.

The truth, when she finds it, is stranger than any ghost story: the entity is not a monster. It is a man in another dimension who does not even know he is doing it. His unresolved anger, his unhealed wounds, his consciousness leaking across the quantum web, poisoning her world like a slow and invisible plague. To stop him, Hailey will have to become something the frontier has never seen -- a warrior of the mind, a healer of broken dimensions, and a woman willing to pay the ultimate price.

"The real battle will be fought inside you."

### Series Badge
A Detective Kiara Estrella Novel

### Status Badge

### Headline
The Silent Witness

### Subtitle Display
A Mystery Thriller

### Book Description (Blurb)

Detective Kiara Estrella grew up watching crime tear her Chicago neighborhood apart. The robbery she witnessed at ten years old left a neighbor dead and left her with a question she has spent her entire life trying to answer: what does justice actually look like when the system that is supposed to deliver it is broken from the inside?

Now a decorated homicide detective, Kiara catches a case that will test everything she believes. An environmental activist is found dead in the wreckage of a factory fire that was no accident. The victim had enemies -- powerful ones -- and the trail of shell companies, laundered money, and silenced witnesses leads straight to the most protected office in the city. The deeper Kiara digs, the more dangerous the game becomes. Not just for her, but for the people she loves most.

The Silent Witness is a mystery thriller about corruption, community, and the cost of doing what is right when every institution around you is designed to make sure you fail. It is a story about a woman who refuses to look away -- and the people who will do anything to make sure she does.

### Target Audience
- Adults 18+
- Readers of mystery/thriller fiction with strong female leads
- Fans of: Tana French, Michael Connelly, Attica Locke
- Readers who connect with themes of systemic corruption, community justice, family loyalty, and personal sacrifice

### Tone Description
Taut, procedural, emotionally grounded. The pacing is relentless but the character work is deep. Chicago as a living, breathing character. Social commentary woven into plot, never preachy.

## SECTION 4: EVERLIGHT KIDS

### Brand Badge
An Everlight Ventures Imprint

### Status Badge

### Headline
Everlight Kids

### Tagline Display
"Every word is a door. And every door is an adventure."

### Brand Description

Everlight Kids is where stories teach without trying and adventures build readers from the ground up.

Our flagship series, Adventures with Sam and Robo, follows a curious boy named Sam and his robot best friend Robo on a five-book journey through the foundations of reading, science, emotional intelligence, and character. Each book introduces a new phonics progression -- from first CVC words to reading fluency -- woven directly into the adventure so that children learn to read the way they learn everything else: by doing something they love.

Every Everlight Kids book is an interactive coloring hybrid. The left page is a black-and-white illustration for coloring. The right page is the same scene in full color. Two books in one. Story time and activity time in a single product. Every title includes CCSS-aligned phonics instruction, CASEL-mapped social-emotional learning, circle time discussion prompts, and a detailed Note for Grown-Ups so parents and teachers can use these books in classrooms, homeschool settings, and family reading time without any additional preparation.

Sam and Robo are just the beginning. The Everlight Kids catalog is growing to include new characters, new series, and new formats -- all built on the same foundation: phonics-forward storytelling, values through character, and books that children reach for again and again.

### Character Spotlight: Sam and Robo

**Sam** is a curious, adventurous boy who discovers a magical book under an old oak tree. Out pops Robo -- a small, friendly robot with kind eyes and a glowing display screen. Together, they rescue animals, explore science labs, solve mysteries, protect the environment, and discover that the greatest superpower of all is the one you share with others. Across five books, Sam earns five superpowers -- not through magic, but through curiosity, kindness, and hard work.

Sam and Robo are the flagship characters of Everlight Kids. They are the foundation the brand is built on, and the friends your children will carry with them long after the last page is turned.

### Target Audience
- Children ages 3-8 (PreK through 2nd grade)
- Parents seeking books that teach phonics AND values without sacrificing story quality
- Teachers and librarians looking for circle-time-ready, curriculum-aligned picture books
- Homeschool families following the Science of Reading
- Gift buyers looking for premium, educational children's books

### Tone Description
Warm, playful, and full of wonder -- but never condescending. The prose respects the intelligence of young readers. The brand voice is that of a trusted teacher who happens to be a great storyteller. Premium without being stiff. Joyful without being saccharine.

### Suggested Accent Color
Warm gold (#E8B84B) for headings and accents. Soft green (#5DAE72) as a secondary. Bright, inviting palette that signals warmth and trust. Think: sunlit backyard, magic glowing from an open book.

## DESIGN NOTES FOR LOVABLE IMPLEMENTATION

### General Layout
- Single-page scroll with anchor navigation (#overview, #beyond-the-veil, #the-silent-witness, #everlight-kids)
- Each section is a full-width block with its own accent color treatment
- Dark theme throughout: background #111111 or #1A1A1A, text #F5F0E8 (warm white, not harsh)
- Section dividers: thin horizontal rule in gold (#C9A84C) or a subtle gradient fade between sections

### Typography Suggestions
- Headings: Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or Libre Baskerville)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Source Sans Pro, or Lato)
- Taglines and pull quotes: Italic serif at a slightly larger size

### Status Badge Styling
- COMING SOON: Amber/gold outlined badge, uppercase, small caps
- IN DEVELOPMENT: Steel blue outlined badge, uppercase, small caps
- LAUNCHING 2026: Warm gold filled badge, uppercase, small caps
- AVAILABLE NOW (future): Green filled badge, uppercase, small caps

### Responsive Notes
- On mobile, book description blurbs should collapse into an expandable "Read More" toggle after the first paragraph
- Cover art images should be full-width on mobile, constrained to ~400px on desktop (placed to the left of the text block)
- CTAs should be full-width buttons on mobile, inline on desktop
