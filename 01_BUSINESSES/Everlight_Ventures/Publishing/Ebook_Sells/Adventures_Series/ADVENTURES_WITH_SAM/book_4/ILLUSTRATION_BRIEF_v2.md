## FORMAT REQUIREMENT -- CRITICAL

Every scene in this book requires **TWO illustrations:**

1. **LEFT PAGE -- B&W Coloring Page:** Clean line art with grey shading. Bold outlines. Open white space for kids to color with crayons/markers. No solid fills except grey shading for depth. This is a COLORING BOOK page.

2. **RIGHT PAGE -- Full Color Illustration:** Same scene, fully colored. Vibrant, saturated, cartoon/animation quality. Rich detail. This is the "finished" version the child sees while coloring the left page.

Both pages show the SAME scene from the SAME angle. The B&W version is not a separate image -- it is the line art version of the color page.

**Total illustrations needed:** 24 (12 scenes x 2 versions each) + cover + back cover

## ART STYLE -- MATCH BOOKS 1-3

The style is established. Do NOT deviate.

- **Genre:** Children's cartoon illustration, coloring book hybrid
- **Line weight:** Bold, clean outlines (B&W pages). Confident strokes, no sketchy lines.
- **Faces:** Large, expressive eyes. Simple noses. Wide mouths that show emotion clearly.
- **Colors (full color pages):** Highly saturated, bright, warm. Think animation-ready. Greens are GREEN. Blues are BLUE. No muted palettes.
- **Backgrounds:** Detailed but not cluttered. Nature scenes should have identifiable elements (individual flowers, leaves, rocks, clouds) that a child can point to and name.
- **Shading (B&W pages):** Use grey tones for depth and dimension. NOT flat line art -- it should look like a professional coloring book page with grey shading to guide the child.

## CHARACTER REFERENCE -- CANONICAL SPECS

### SAM
- **Age appearance:** 5-7 years old
- **Hair:** Brown/dark, messy, spiky on top (outside-kid hair)
- **Eyes:** Large, round, brown, VERY expressive -- the first thing you notice
- **Face:** Round, warm, animated. Big smile when excited. Brow furrows when thinking.
- **Skin:** Warm, sun-touched (match Books 1-3 exactly)
- **Outfit for Book 4:** Red t-shirt (consistent with series), jeans, scuffed sneakers
- **Backpack:** Slightly too big for him, worn, always on his back
- **Superpower moment (Ch 5):** Add a short cape flowing behind him and a golden-green glow aura. Match the "superhero reveal" style from Book 3's Chapter 5 celebration scene.

### ROBO
- **Size:** Sam's height or slightly shorter, compact
- **Body:** Boxy/rounded torso, silver/grey metallic with blue and orange accent lights
- **Head:** Rounded square shape with a small antenna on top (ball on stick)
- **Eyes:** Square/rounded with horizontal line pupils (like screen displays). Glow blue-orange.
- **Chest display:** Rectangular screen panel on torso showing icons and simple graphics (changes per scene -- see scene notes below)
- **Limbs:** Simple, rounded, non-threatening. Feet are flat/boot-like.
- **Expression:** Robo emotes through his EYES and CHEST DISPLAY. No mouth. Eyes change shape to show curiosity (wide), concern (angled), joy (curved up).
- **Movement:** Upright posture, sometimes tilts head. Occasionally raises one arm to point or wave.

### LILY
- **Hair:** Short, dark, sometimes in a ponytail
- **Eyes:** Large, bright, friendly
- **Outfit:** Casual -- overalls or t-shirt and shorts. Energetic kid.
- **Role:** Sam's friend who kicks off the adventure. Appears in Ch 1 and Ch 5.

### MR. GREEN (NEW -- Chapter 2)
- **Age:** 50s-60s, older and kind
- **Build:** Average, slightly stocky
- **Face:** Warm smile, laugh lines, kind eyes
- **Outfit:** Work apron over casual clothes, work gloves, maybe a cap
- **Setting:** Surrounded by recycling bins and organized materials
- **Vibe:** Friendly neighborhood mentor. The person who actually does the work.

### FARMER BROWN (NEW -- Chapter 3)
- **Age:** 50s-60s, weathered and sturdy
- **Build:** Broad-shouldered, strong hands
- **Face:** Sun-tanned, squint lines from being outdoors, warm and patient
- **Outfit:** Wide-brimmed hat, work boots, flannel or denim, rolled sleeves
- **Setting:** His farm with rows of crops, barn in background
- **Vibe:** A man who works with the earth every day and respects it deeply.

### RANGER JANE (NEW -- Chapter 4)
- **Age:** 40s, strong and capable
- **Build:** Athletic, confident posture
- **Face:** Warm but serious when needed. Eyes that have seen a lot of forest.
- **Outfit:** Ranger uniform -- hat, khaki/green shirt, badge, boots
- **Setting:** The forest, surrounded by trees and wildlife
- **Vibe:** She belongs here. The forest is her responsibility and she takes it personally.

### THE HERON (recurring visual element)
- **Appears in:** Chapter 1 (at the dirty riverbank) and Chapter 5 (far below, stepping into cleaner water)
- **Design:** Elegant, long-legged wading bird. Grey-blue feathers. Long neck.
- **Posture:** Perfectly still in Ch 1 (waiting). Moving into water in Ch 5 (the payoff).
- **Style:** More realistic than the cartoon characters -- she is the "real" element in a cartoon world. Dignified.

## SCENE-BY-SCENE ILLUSTRATION SPECS

Each scene gets a LEFT (B&W coloring) and RIGHT (full color) page.

---

### SCENE 1 -- Chapter 1 Opening: Lily Arrives
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 1, opening

**Composition:** Sam and Robo stand at their front door/porch. Lily runs toward them down a path. Morning light. Trees and flowers line the path. Sam has backpack on. Sunny neighborhood setting.

**Sam:** Curious expression, looking at Lily running toward them
**Robo chest display:** Sunrise/sun icon
**Lily:** Mid-run, arms waving, urgent expression

**B&W notes:** Clean neighborhood scene with lots of elements to color -- house, path, flowers, trees, clouds, sun. Open sky area for easy coloring.
**Color notes:** Warm morning palette. Bright blue sky, green trees, colorful flowers. Sam's red shirt pops.

---

### SCENE 2 -- Chapter 1: The Dirty Riverbank
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 1, discovering the pollution

**Composition:** Wide view of the riverbank. Sam, Robo, and Lily stand at the top looking down. Trash visible -- plastic bottles, netting, styrofoam cup in reeds. Murky water. A fish peeks above the surface looking sad. The heron stands still at the water's edge.

**Sam:** Concerned face, brow furrowed
**Robo chest display:** Small downward arrow
**Lily:** Pointing at the mess

**B&W notes:** Lots of individual trash items to spot and color. Water ripples. Plants along the bank. The heron should be clearly visible but not dominating.
**Color notes:** The river is grey-green and murky (NOT the bright blue of healthy water). Land around it still has healthy greens -- contrast between sick river and healthy land. Trash in muted tones against the natural colors.

---

### SCENE 3 -- Chapter 1: Cleaning Up Together
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 1, the cleanup

**Composition:** Sam reaching into reeds with gloves on. Robo holding a trash bag open. Lily picking up a plastic bottle. The heron watches nearby. Fish visible in water. Riverbank is half-cleaned -- one side still messy, the other getting better.

**Sam:** Determined, dirt on his shirt, working hard
**Robo chest display:** Small checkmark icon
**Lily:** Smiling, contributing

**B&W notes:** Action scene with lots of detail -- different trash items, plants, water, gloves, bags. Each character doing something different.
**Color notes:** Sam's red shirt has dirt smudges (he's working). Warm afternoon light. The cleaned side of the bank shows healthier colors than the dirty side.

---

### SCENE 4 -- Chapter 2: Mr. Green's Recycling Center
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 2, arriving at the center

**Composition:** Sam and Robo in front of the recycling center building. Mr. Green stands in the doorway smiling, wearing apron and gloves. Four large bins visible behind him, each with a symbol and label: PAPER, PLASTIC, METAL, GLASS. Sign on building: "GREEN RECYCLING CENTER."

**Sam:** Excited, looking up at Mr. Green
**Robo chest display:** Small recycling symbol (three arrows in triangle)

**B&W notes:** The four bins should be clearly outlined with their symbols big enough for kids to identify and color. Building exterior with sign. Bold lettering on bins.
**Color notes:** Bins are distinct bright colors -- blue (paper), yellow (plastic), red (metal), green (glass). Mr. Green's apron is earth-toned. Building is clean and welcoming.

---

### SCENE 5 -- Chapter 2: Sorting and Learning
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 2, inside the center

**Composition:** Inside the recycling center. Sam placing a bottle in the plastic bin. Robo scanning an item with a light from his display. Mr. Green supervising and pointing. Table with various recyclable items: cans, bottles, newspapers, cardboard. Conveyor belt in background. Poster on wall: "REDUCE. REUSE. RECYCLE."

**Sam:** Focused, learning, holding a bottle
**Robo chest display:** Thumbs-up icon

**B&W notes:** Many individual small objects on the table for kids to identify and color -- cans, bottles, paper, cardboard boxes. The poster text should be readable. Good "I Spy" opportunity.
**Color notes:** Interior is bright and organized. Each recyclable item is clearly colored to match its bin. The conveyor belt adds a sense of industrial/STEM wonder. Warm indoor lighting.

---

### SCENE 6 -- Chapter 3: Farmer Brown's Farm
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 3, arriving at the farm

**Composition:** Farm scene. Farmer Brown stands next to rows of plants with Sam and Robo. Drip irrigation tubes visible along plant rows. Background: red barn, windmill, rain barrel collecting water from a roof gutter. Butterfly on a flower. Sun bright overhead.

**Sam:** Crouching to look at the irrigation tubes up close, curious
**Robo chest display:** Water droplet icon
**Farmer Brown:** Standing proud, wide hat, gesturing at his farm

**B&W notes:** Classic farm scene with lots of coloring opportunity -- barn, windmill, rows of plants, irrigation tubes, rain barrel, animals (butterfly, bird). Each vegetable row can be a different plant.
**Color notes:** Lush, green, thriving farm. Red barn pops. Sky is brilliant blue. Vegetables are vibrant -- red tomatoes, green lettuce, orange carrots visible in rows. Earth tones on Farmer Brown. Everything feels healthy and cared-for.

---

### SCENE 7 -- Chapter 3: Rain Barrels and Leak Detection
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 3, working with water systems

**Composition:** Sam connecting a tube from the rain barrel to a garden bed. Robo scanning a pipe with a blue beam from his display, detecting a leak (shown with small water drops spraying from the pipe). Farmer Brown watches approvingly, hand on Sam's shoulder. Plants and vegetables surround them. A small bird drinks from a puddle nearby.

**Sam:** Hands dirty from soil, concentrating on the connection
**Robo chest display:** Scanning beam emanating toward the leaky pipe

**B&W notes:** The rain barrel, pipes, and water drops should be clearly drawn. Lots of garden elements -- plants, tools (watering can, trowel), vegetables. The leak should be obvious with droplets.
**Color notes:** Late afternoon golden light. Rain barrel is dark blue. Water sparkles. Vegetables are colorful. Sam has dirt on his hands and knees (he doesn't mind). Robo's scanning beam is soft blue.

---

### SCENE 8 -- Chapter 4: The Forest and Ranger Jane
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 4, entering the forest

**Composition:** Forest edge. Sam, Robo, and Ranger Jane stand at a clearing. Tall trees around them. Animals visible: deer peeking from behind a tree, squirrel on a branch, bird in a nest, rabbit near bushes. In background, a section shows stumps where trees were cut -- the damaged area. Ranger Jane gestures toward the forest.

**Sam:** Looking serious, taking it in
**Robo chest display:** Small tree icon
**Ranger Jane:** Strong posture, arm extended, showing them the forest

**B&W notes:** Rich forest scene. Individual animals should be clearly visible and fun to find and color. The tree stumps area in the background should be noticeably bare compared to the lush forest. Lots of leaves, branches, mushrooms, flowers on the forest floor.
**Color notes:** Deep greens and golden dappled light in the healthy forest. The stump area is slightly grey/brown -- a visual wound. Animals have bright eyes. Ranger Jane's green uniform blends with the forest she belongs to.

---

### SCENE 9 -- Chapter 4: Planting Trees
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 4, the tree planting

**Composition:** Sam digs a hole with a small shovel. Robo holds a young sapling carefully. Ranger Jane demonstrates planting technique. Animals watch from around them -- the deer, rabbit family, birds in remaining trees. Hand-painted wooden sign reads: "WILDLIFE ZONE -- PLEASE RESPECT OUR HOME." New saplings already planted in a row.

**Sam:** On his knees in dirt, digging, happy and determined
**Robo chest display:** Growing plant animation (small seedling with upward arrow)
**Ranger Jane:** Guiding, smiling, approving

**B&W notes:** Lots of detail in the planting scene -- shovel, soil, sapling with roots, the sign with readable text. Animals watching should be fun to find and color. The row of already-planted saplings gives structure to the scene.
**Color notes:** Warm, hopeful afternoon light. Fresh soil is rich brown. Saplings have bright green leaves. The watching animals look peaceful and grateful. Contrast between the healing hillside (new saplings) and the healthy forest in background. This scene should feel like progress.

---

### SCENE 10 -- Chapter 5: The Mountain Hike
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 5, hiking up

**Composition:** Sam, Robo, Lily, and friends hiking up a mountain trail. Path winds through wildflowers and tall grass. Rocks and boulders line the trail. A stream runs alongside. Sam leads with his backpack. Mountain peak visible in distance with a faint golden-green glow near the top. Birds fly overhead.

**Sam:** Leading the way, determined, excited
**Robo chest display:** Compass arrow pointing up
**Lily:** Following behind Sam, smiling

**B&W notes:** Sweeping nature scene. Wildflowers of different types along the trail (fun to color different colors). Rocks, stream with ripples, clouds, birds. The mountain dominates the background. The faint glow at the top should be suggested with light rays.
**Color notes:** Glorious color. Wildflowers in every color -- purple, yellow, red, white. Sky is brilliant blue with puffy clouds. Mountain is majestic green and grey. The faint glow near the peak is golden-green. This scene should feel like the start of something big.

---

### SCENE 11 -- Chapter 5: The Crystal Cave
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 5, discovering the crystal

**Composition:** The hidden cave at the mountain peak. Crystal sits on a natural stone pedestal inside, radiating golden-green light. Sam stands at the entrance, one hand reaching toward the crystal, face full of awe. Robo beside him, display glowing. Cave walls have stalactites, crystal formations, moss. Through the cave entrance, the entire valley is visible: the river, the farm, the forest, the town. Everything connects.

**Sam:** Reaching toward crystal, face shows awe (not triumph -- something deeper)
**Robo chest display:** Mirrors the crystal's glow -- bright golden-green

**B&W notes:** MOST IMPORTANT B&W PAGE. The crystal should have dramatic light rays. Cave formations give lots of texture to color. The valley view through the entrance should show recognizable locations from earlier chapters (river, barn, forest). This is the "wow" coloring page.
**Color notes:** MOST IMPORTANT COLOR PAGE. The crystal glows golden-green -- the color of nature itself. Cave sparkles with minerals. Through the entrance, the entire landscape is in golden-hour light. Every location from the book is visible and connected. This is the culmination image.

---

### SCENE 12 -- Chapter 5: The Superpower Moment
**Manuscript location:** Chapter 5, the celebration/transformation

**Composition:** Sam on the mountain peak, arms outstretched, eyes closed or looking up with joy. A golden-green glow aura surrounds him. Short cape flowing behind him (matching Books 1-3 superpower reveal style). Nature responds: birds fly in a circle above him, flowers bloom at his feet, trees lean toward him. Robo beside him celebrating, display showing a nature symbol. Lily and friends watch in amazement. Valley stretches behind them. The heron from Chapter 1 is visible far below, finally stepping into the water.

**Sam:** Pure joy and connection. Cape. Glow aura. This is his hero moment.
**Robo chest display:** Glowing nature symbol (tree + sun + water combined)
**Reference:** Match the energy and composition of Book 3's Chapter 5 celebration scene (image 3_5.jpg) -- Sam center stage, Robo beside him, friends around them, dynamic energy lines, confetti/celebration elements. But instead of confetti, use NATURE elements: leaves, petals, birds, light rays.

**B&W notes:** MOST DYNAMIC B&W PAGE. Energy lines radiating from Sam. Birds in spiral above. Flowers blooming at feet. This should look like the most exciting coloring page in the book -- the payoff. Match the celebration energy from Book 3's finale.
**Color notes:** Full explosion of color. Golden-green aura on Sam. Every color of nature represented. Birds create a colorful spiral. Sam's face shows pure joy. Far below, the heron steps into the water -- a small, quiet detail that closes the loop from Chapter 1. The whole image says: everything is connected.
